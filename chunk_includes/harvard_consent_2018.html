WELCOME!

Please read this consent agreement carefully before deciding whether to participate in this study.

Purpose of the research: 
To answer questions about audio recordings presented to you at various speeds.

What we will ask you to do: 
In this experiment, you will hear 36 sentences, one at a time. Some of these sentences will be played at a faster speed. Your job is to listen carefully to the sentence and then answer a question about what you heard. This task is estimated to take about 20 minutes.

Risks and benefits: 
There are no anticipated risks associated with participating in this study. There are no anticipated benefits to the participant beyond whatever amusement you happen to derive from the task.

Selection of participants: 
To participate in this study, you must be 18 years of age and a native speaker of American English.

Compensation: 
Your compensation for completing this task will be $2.00. This is what you agreed to when selecting this Amazon Mechanical Turk HIT.

Confidentiality: 
Your participation in this study will remain confidential. We do not have access to your identity.

Participation and withdrawal: 
Your participation in this study is completely voluntary, and you may quit at any time without penalty or loss of benefit to which you may otherwise be entitled.
